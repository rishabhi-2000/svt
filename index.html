About SEVENTEEN

SEVENTEEN are a 13-member South Korean boy group formed by Pledis Entertainment. The group is split into three sub-units — Hip-Hop Unit, Vocal Unit, and Performance Unit — and are celebrated for their tight choreography, catchy songs, and hands-on creative input. Over the years they have released multiple albums and toured worldwide, growing a massive global fanbase.

Achievements & Awards

SEVENTEEN have won numerous awards across Korea and internationally, including multiple Album of the Year quarter awards, Best Male Group recognitions, and other major industry prizes. They have also completed multiple world tours and sold out stadium shows.
