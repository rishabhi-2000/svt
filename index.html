About SEVENTEEN

SEVENTEEN is a 13-member South Korean self-producing boy group formed by Pledis Entertainment. The group's name expresses "13 members + 3 teams + 1 group." They debuted in 2015 and their fandom is known as CARAT.

Debut: May 26, 2015
Label: Pledis (HYBE label family)
Fans: CARAT
Members: 13

Notable achievements

  • Multiple-million selling albums and major domestic & international awards.
  • First K-pop act to perform Glastonbury's Pyramid Stage (2024).
  • Recognized by Korean government cultural awards for contribution to pop culture.
